About Uttorer Karukaj

Authentic Handmade Crafts from Northern Bangladesh — Preserving heritage, empowering artisans, celebrating tradition.

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Northern Bangladesh, Uttorer Karukaj began with a simple yet profound vision: to preserve and celebrate the centuries-old craftsmanship of rural artisans from Rangpur, Dinajpur, and the surrounding regions.

What started as a small community initiative has grown into a brand that connects talented artisans with craft lovers worldwide. Each bamboo basket, jute product, and traditional handicraft tells a unique story — of patience, skill, and cultural heritage passed down through generations.

We believe in the beauty of imperfection, the value of handmade, and the importance of sustainable practices. Every purchase directly supports local families, preserves dying art forms, and promotes eco-friendly materials that honor our planet.
